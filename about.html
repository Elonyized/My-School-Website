About Our School

Learn more about who we are

Greenland academy was found in 1995 with a vision to provide quality education to every child regardless of background.

Over the years we grown from a small school of 50 students to over 2000 students today.

Our Mission

To provide quality and affordable education that prepares a student for the future.

Our Vision

To be the leading school in producing well rounded and responsible citizens.
